Start Classifying Brain Tumors

Now you can start classifying both MR Images and Whole Slide Images from us.
You can analyse by yourself how the tumor regions are affected inside the brain and continue with the WSI classification through this website.

What this system do?

This system will segment MRI and classify both MRI and WSI images into 3 tumor classes, glioblastoma, oligodendroglioma and astrocytoma.
After classifying images, this will generate heatmaps on both MRI and WSI image to identify affected tumor areas clearly.

3D MRI Segmentation

Data acquired from 3D MRI volumes will be labelled to 3 isolate regions of interests, Edema, Non-enhancing tumor, Enhancing tumor and displayed.

3D MRI Classification and Interpretable Machine Learning

Interpretability will be applied to the 3D MRI images using GradCAM.
GradCAM will generate heatmaps which overlays on MRI identifying tumor regions.

WSI Classification

We will classify WSI images into same 3 tumor regions and will generate Heatmaps overlaid on WSI images to analyze affected areas clearly.
